Supplier Manual

Last updated: Apr 23rd, 2017

Get account

Request account

Navigate to https://highpass.aero/Supplier/Auth/RequestAccount and fill in information about your company:

Sign agreement with HighPass

After you submitted your request, our manager will contact you for an agreement.

Login

You ill receive an email once your account is created with the credentials. Navigate to https://highpass.aero and login with the credentials sent.

Change your password

We strongly recommend you to change the password you received in email for stronger account security.

Manage services

Create or edit service

In order to create a service:
  • Select service category the Services item in the left menu:
  • Click on the Create button or the Edit one

Service Details


Service Rules


# Parameter Description
1 Terminal A terminal in which the service is provided. Create a new terminal if there is no such terminal in the list.
2 Pre-order Period A time limit before the service provision to create an order.
3 Price A service price in Euro currency. Can be configured per different age groups (adults, children and infants) as well as the groups ages.
4 Direction Please select corresponding options available for the service

Facilities (for business lounge only)

Please select facilities available in your lounge and specify if each facility either free or chargeable

Unavailability Periods

In case if the service provision is planned to be stopped for a while in the future, please specify corresponding periods to block ordering during that time.

Working Days

Please specify your working schedule to avoid booking your service out of working time.

Delete service

In order to delete a service:
  • Select service category in the Services item in the left menu:
  • Click on the Delete button

Warning!

Deleting a service will also delete all active orders that have not yet been completed.

Manage orders

View orders

Navigate to Orders items in the left menu, to view your orders:

Export orders

To export orders into .csv file, do the following:
  • Filter orders to get ones you are going to export
  • Press Export button

Accept order

Orders in the orders list are marked as new until you accept them. To accept an order, do the following:
  • Select checkbox near the order(s) you want to accept
  • Select Accept in bulk action list above the orders table
  • Click on Apply button

Complete order

Completing an order means, you provided it to the customer. In order to mark an order as completed in web application, do the following:
  • Click on the Complete button at the order row in the table
  • Enter booking code received from the customer in the pop-up window
  • Click on the Complete button

Cancel order

To cancel order, do the following:
  • Click on the Cancel button at the order row in the table
  • Specify cancellation reason in the pop-up window. Please not that it will be sent to the customer
  • Click on the Cancel button

View supplier account

To get information on available services, fees and payment information, please refer to the Supplier Account in the left menu. In case if any information should be changed, please contact HighPass.
