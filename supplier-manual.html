Supplier Manual

Last updated: Apr 23rd, 2017

Get account

Request account

Navigate to https://easypass.aero/Supplier/Auth/RequestAccount and fill in information about your company:

Sign agreement with EasyPass

After you submitted your request, our manager will contact you for an agreement.

Login

You ill receive an email once your account is created with the credentials. Navigate to https://easypass.aero and login with the credentials sent.

Change your password

We strongly recommend you to change the password you received in email for stronger account security.
